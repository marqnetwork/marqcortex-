1️⃣ SECTION A — Executive Diagnostic Overview (Top Block)

📌 Purpose: Immediate clarity in 20 seconds.

Layout: 2-Column Grid

LEFT COLUMN (Primary Signal Card)

• AI Readiness Score (large)
• Category (Manual / Fragmented / AI-Ready)
• Confidence Level
• Growth Risk Indicator

RIGHT COLUMN (Diagnostic Summary)

• Primary Bottleneck Theme
• Secondary Bottleneck
• Estimated Annual Impact Range
• Summary Narrative (3–5 lines max)

⚠️ This replaces your current short “Core Problems” intro.

It must feel like:
Executive intelligence snapshot.

2️⃣ SECTION B — Primary Bottleneck Deep Dive

📌 Purpose: Explain WHY it exists.

Each bottleneck becomes a structured block.

Layout: Expandable Cards (Stacked)

Each card contains 6 structured layers:

B1 — Bottleneck Header

• Title
• Priority (Immediate / High / Moderate)
• Impact Area (Revenue / Ops / Risk)
• Confidence Strength (Weak / Moderate / Strong Pattern)

B2 — Structured Explanation

Short paragraph (4–6 lines max).

Not one sentence.
Not a novel.
Clear reasoning.

B3 — Evidence Mapping

Show:

• Question reference
• Client answer (short excerpt)
• AI interpretation

Stack 3–5 evidence items per bottleneck.

This is where depth lives.

B4 — Root Cause Classification

System-level tag (not surface symptom).

Example:

Root Cause Type:
• Workflow design failure
• Tool fragmentation
• Manual dependency
• Governance gap
• Data flow breakdown

This prevents shallow conclusions.

B5 — Impact Projection

If unaddressed:

• Growth ceiling at X%
• Revenue loss potential
• Headcount inflation risk
• SLA breakdown risk

Concrete projection.

B6 — AI Intervention Direction

Not a sales pitch.
Just structured intervention paths:

Short-Term
Mid-Term
Long-Term

Repeat this format for:

Primary bottleneck
Secondary bottleneck
Tertiary issue (if needed)
